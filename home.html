Conscious Craft

NovofitBD was born from a desire for refined simplicity. We create foundational pieces with a focus on quality materials, ethical production, and timeless design. Each garment is a testament to thoughtful craftsmanship, made to be cherished for years to come.

Frequently Asked Questions

We offer free returns within 30 days of purchase for all unworn and unwashed items. The product must be in its original packaging with all tags attached. Refunds will be processed within 5-7 business days of us receiving the return.

We currently do not offer international shipping, and orders can only be delivered within Bangladesh, but we are working to expand our shipping services in the future.

Please refer to our detailed sizing guide available on each product page. Our garments generally follow a modern, relaxed fit. If you're between sizes, we recommend sizing up for a more comfortable fit.

We are committed to ethical and sustainable practices. We use a variety of eco-friendly materials such as organic cotton and recycled polyester, and partner with factories that adhere to fair labor standards.
